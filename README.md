# PM & AI Learning Hub

A self-contained, interactive learning platform for transitioning into **AI Product Management**.

Open `index.html` in any browser — no build step, no dependencies.

## What's inside
- **Lessons** — 23 lessons across 6 tracks (AI PM, PM foundations, agentic AI & LLM literacy, leadership, career, security & cloud)
- **Progress** — completion tracking and next-lesson nudges
- **Roles** — real AI PM job postings mapped to a weighted "Top Skills in Demand" panel
- **Build Log** — a portfolio of hands-on AI PM artifacts to ship
- **Eval Lab** — a step-by-step walkthrough to build a real eval harness
- **Interview** — an AI PM question bank with answer scaffolds
- **Teardowns** — AI product dissections through the PM lens
- **Books & References** — curated study guides and reading list
